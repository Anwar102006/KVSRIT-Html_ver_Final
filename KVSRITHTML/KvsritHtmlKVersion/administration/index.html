Administrative Structure

Roles & Responsibilities

Administrative Roles

Principal

  • Looks after all Administrative and Academic activities as per AICTE, JNTUA and Government of AP norms.
  • Conducts all UG, PG programmes according to affiliating university guidelines.
  • Appoints Faculty Members as per AICTE and university norms.
  • Acts as Chief Warden for Gents and Ladies Hostels.
  • Monitors admissions, class work, placements, R&D activities, and discipline.
  • Monitors smooth conduct of Quality Management System in accordance with NBA and NAAC.

Head of Department (HOD)

  • Responsible for all academic affairs of the Department.
  • Looks after day-to-day activities of teaching and non-teaching staff.
  • Reports requirements (Faculty, equipment, books & journals) to the Principal.
  • Looks after R&D, Consultancy and Research Publications.
  • Arranges Guest Lectures, Seminars, Workshops, and Conferences.
  • Responsible for innovative programmes including collaboration with industries.
  • Responsible for students mentoring system.

Training & Placement Officer

  • Responsible for all activities relating to student placement.
  • Coordinates with industries for vocational training courses.
  • Arranges guest lectures, workshops, seminars, industrial visits & educational tours.
  • Coordinates on-campus and off-campus interviews for meritorious students.
  • Responsible for interaction with industries for functioning of EDPs.

Research & Development Head

  • Guide and monitor the Institution's R&D activities including Student and Faculty Research Publications.
  • Direct the R&D program, from new product proposal to commercialization.
  • Drive accountability, consistency, quality, and process standardization across all phases of R&D.
  • Develop and monitor research plans and budgets for R&D projects.

Library In-charge

  • Responsible for the overall in-charge of the library.
  • Maintains documentation of books, journals, magazines, newspapers, CDs & library materials.
  • Prepares periodical requirements of books and journals for students and Faculty.
  • Responsible for maintaining and updating e-journals and all teaching aids.
  • Arranges periodic inventory of library materials.

Administrative Officer

  • Takes care of student scholarships (first graduate, SC, ST, MBC).
  • Maintains Faculty leave records (casual leave, vacation, on duty & permission).
  • Acts as Coordinator for all college maintenance activities.
  • Takes care of HR policies inside and outside the College.
  • Conducts interviews as per HOD requirements with selection committee.
  • Handles admission approval procedure and university communication.

Physical Education Director

  • Responsible for all activities related to Physical Education.
  • Arranges physical fitness camp for students and staff.
  • Responsible for procurement and maintenance of sports goods and play fields.
  • Coordinates Intra College, Inter College, Inter University and Inter State competitions.

Hostel Warden

  • Responsible for allotment of rooms to students.
  • Responsible for maintenance of Hostel.
  • Looks after the quality of food served in the hostels.
  • Keeps strict discipline in incoming and outgoing of students.
  • Reports to the Principal in case of indiscipline or misbehaviour.
  • Arranges for First-Aid in case of emergency and hospitalization.
